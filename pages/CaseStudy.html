MITRE CALDERA® Case Study

The framework has been tested with MITRE CALDERA®. The

"Overall Score"

obtained by evaluating the mean over all the categories of the framework is

43%

.

From the results we can see that the tool has a performance that is very different based on the category (

note also the table below

), for example it has a very good "Organization" result (

90%

), this is due to the fact that tool is based on the MITRE ATT&CK framework that is very well structured. It is also important to point out that the analysis framework itself has been developed by studying MITRE ATT&CK, therefore this could be a biased result.

It has a notable performace also for the "Attack" and "Simulation" categories (

69%

and

71%

), reflecting the good capabilities of the software to help red team simulations.

On the other hand it has insufficient performance on the "Results" and "Information and Filtering" categories (respectively

28%

,

31%

).
